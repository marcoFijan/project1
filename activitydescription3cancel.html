Hey! Come join us at our studysession in the Mac. We do this every month and it's pretty fun. Good food, nice seats and just good fun. We are studying Informatics but everyone is welcome. Perhaps you can teach us something new with your study! Most of the time it's not a problem to claim a few seats in the mac so long we buy something there. One cup of coffee is enough. But if it becomes too busy we have to leave. But we've never had that problem. So, what are you waiting for? Join!
